## Collection Rules

- Store source URL, domain, fetched title if available, and analysis categories.
- Do not store or copy full copyrighted articles, thumbnails, images, or character designs.
- Use sources for abstract safety and trend insights only.
- Treat generated output as prototype material until a human creator redraws, edits, and approves the final art.

## Learning Cycle Outputs

When the app runs repeated learning, it writes:

- `daily_learning_report.json`: category frequency, legal risk distribution, next-generation advice
- `round_analyses.json`: each round's raw analysis
- `evolution_memory_snapshot.json`: current accumulated memory
